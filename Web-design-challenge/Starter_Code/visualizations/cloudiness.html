Cloudiness

The graph above compares the cloudiness of different cities according to their latitude. The graph above seems to have no interpretable trend from the data. This lack of a trend is most likely due to the fact that there are a wide variety of reasons that clouds form. Cloudiness of an area can be affected by elevation, vicinity to water, average temperature, overall precipitation, and many other factors. It should also be noted that these values are only for the day of 01/05/17 so it does not the cloudiness for each city throughout the year.
