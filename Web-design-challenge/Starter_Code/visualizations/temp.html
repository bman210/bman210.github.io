Max Temperature

In this graph we looked at the max temperature reach in each city as compared to latitude. This graph is probably the easiest to interpret as it shows most cities above the equator have a lower max temp and cities below have a higher max temp. Seeing as all these temperatures were taken on 01/05/17 it would be winter for the northern hemisphere and summer for the southern hemisphere. The earth's 23.5 degree tilt on its axis leads to seasonal temperature changes as seen in this graph.
