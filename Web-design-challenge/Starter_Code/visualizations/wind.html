Wind Speed

In this graph we took a look at winds speeds by city in comparison to latitude. Overall most cities seem to have lower max wind speeds, but as you get further away from the equator there seems to be an uptick in max wind speeds. Specifically, there seems to be an uptick in wind speeds between 40 to 60 degrees latitude. This is most likely due to the varying mountainous regions in this latitude for example the Rocky Mountains in north America or the Himalayas in Asia.
